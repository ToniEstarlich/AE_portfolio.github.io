Cookie Policy

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are essential for the functioning of many websites and help us improve your experience on our site.

What Cookies Do We Use?

We use cookies for analytical purposes and to enhance your experience on our website. These include cookies for Google Analytics and other tracking services that help us understand how users interact with our site.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies, but please note that some features of the website may not work properly without them.
