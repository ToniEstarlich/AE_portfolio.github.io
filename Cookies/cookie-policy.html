Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help websites function properly and improve your browsing experience. They do not harm your system and are often necessary for website functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to work correctly. Without these cookies, services such as navigation and access to secure areas cannot function.
  • Analytical Cookies: We may use tools such as Google Analytics to anonymously collect information about how visitors use the website. This helps us understand performance and improve content.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Some pages may contain embedded content from third parties (e.g., YouTube, social media). These services may set their own cookies on your device.

Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR, we may store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this site. For all other types of cookies, we require your explicit consent.

Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can modify your browser settings to refuse, accept, or delete cookies. However, please note that some features of this website may not work as intended without cookies.

For more information, visit: www.aboutcookies.org

Consent

When you visit this website for the first time, you may be presented with a banner requesting your consent to use non-essential cookies. By clicking "Accept" or continuing to use the website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

Updates to This Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the law or technology. Please revisit this page regularly to stay informed.
